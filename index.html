How to decrypt
1
Scan the QR code
Use any QR scanner app to read the encrypted payload, then paste it in the field on the left.
2
Enter the shared key
The sender must share the secret key with you securely (e.g. verbally or via a separate encrypted channel).
3
Verify & decrypt
The app automatically verifies the SHA-256 integrity hash after decryption to confirm the message is untampered.
SECURITY NOTE
Never share the secret key through the same channel as the QR code. Use a separate, trusted medium.

AES-256-CBC

Military-grade symmetric encryption with PKCS7 padding and a random IV per message.

PBKDF2 Key Derivation

Your password is stretched into a 256-bit key using 200,000 iterations of HMAC-SHA256.

SHA-256 Integrity

Every payload includes a hash of the original message to detect any tampering during transit.
